Francis started his career as university instructor at Mindanao State University - Iligan Institute of Technology (MSU-IIT) in 2003. He started as junior instructor in the same department he earned his degree Bachelor of Science in Information Technology major in Business Software Development. After a year, he was then awarded the tenure. In his tenure he taught several core IT subjects mostly towards software development.

He was also actively engaged with continuous improvements of which he became a member of the Mindanao Association for Quality (MAQ), a not-for-profit organization assisting several companies and organizations, both government and private, to establish Quality Circles (QC). It was during this time that he actively advocated for 5S, Kaizen, and Six Sigma.

His interest in healthcare lead him to pursue nursing degree which he earned in 2009, and on the same year, he passed the Nursing Licensure Examination awarding him as a Registered Nurse.

With his new distinct combination of skill set, he then crossed trained and hired to teach in the College of Nursing in MSU-IIT. During this period he had the opportunity establish the first Nursing Informatics Laboratory in Mindanao and held the first trainor's training for Nursing Informatics program, a program he single-handedly developed.

After a decade in the academe, he decided to transition and work full-time with St. Lukes Medical Center Global City where he worked as Clinical Systems Analyst focusing in integrating electronic medical test results into a centralized repository generated from various autonomous machines using HL7.

Driven by his passion in leading teams, he pursued the career in project management and joined LBC Express as IT Project Manager. During this time he took the leadership in delivering key improvements from the Point-Of-Sale Systems used in all 1,200 branches then to the distribution and sorting facilities until the it gets delivered to the recipient which is validated with a handheld device.

In 2014, Francis decided to move to Canada as a landed immigrant seeking new life experiences. He first landed in Winnipeg where he worked as Support Worker for young adults with special needs. He then took the role as Technical Support Representative in Shaw which gave him the opportunity to speak with Canadian customers all-over Canada.

Then in 2016, he moved to Calgary to join the engineering team of Shaw as Test Analyst. His previous experience in writing and understanding codes came handy and was given the opportunity to lead a small team of integration testers.

At the moment, he is residing in Toronto where he took the role as IT Project Coordinator at Freedom Mobile HQ.
